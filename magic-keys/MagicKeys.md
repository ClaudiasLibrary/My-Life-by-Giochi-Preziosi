# Magic Keys

There are 66 Magic Keys.
You can unlock two types of content: items and clothing.

## Items
Insert your Magic Key into the cartridge slot to equip your avatar with new accessories.
The accessories unlocked through the Magic Keys can be found directly in your item list, accessible by pressing the corresponding button.
You can send your new items to other inhabitants of the My Life world or keep them to improve the level of your needs.

## Clothing
Insert your Magic Key into the cartridge slot to equip your avatar with new outfits.
You will find the new clothes directly in your virtual wardrobe, and you will be free to wear them.
You can also find the new clothes in the SPECIAL section of the shop.
The infrared port allows you to send your new clothes to your friends in the real world.
